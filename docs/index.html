A protocol that enables users to take “steps” among three different aspects of crypto just like dancing waltz.

Three Steps in Waltz Economy

For Crypto Traders Lending for Tax saving

Users are able to borrow zETH (wrapped ETH with which holders receive WLZ tokens) by pledging ETH as collateral and borrowing ETH by pledging zETH as collateral.
With this mechanism, there is no overcollateralization nor liquidation of collateral.

Learn more

For DeFi Farmers Effective Farming of WALTZ Token

By providing liquidity to zETH and its paired assets of a certain DEX, users receive much more WLZ tokens as a reward.

Learn more

For ETH HODLer Fixed Rate Bond

Users are able to receive fixed rate interest by paying zETH (or rather staking it) in the contract. The interest of fixed rate bond is relatively high comparing with just holding zETH. The number of fixed rate bond is limited and you need to hold WLZ tokens to purchase it.

Learn more

Governance

Important aspects such as the parameter or the interests and the interest rate of the fixed rate bond are decided by the governance mechanism of the protocol.
